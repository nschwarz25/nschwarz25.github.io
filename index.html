Famous BLT's Right Down the Street

Whether you just found us or you’re an ‘ole timer, we’re glad you’re here! SIMPLY THE BEST FOOD IN TOWN and striving to be better every single day. ​ World famous for our BLT’s, we’re sooo much more… Merritt’s Grill is part of the fabric of Chapel Hill. Since introducing our famous BLT in 1992, our food, family, friends and partners have been acclaimed and well documented in hundreds of southern food magazines, television food shows, articles and books. Today, Merritt’s Grill continues to build on it’s heritage by holding onto the old while embracing the new!
